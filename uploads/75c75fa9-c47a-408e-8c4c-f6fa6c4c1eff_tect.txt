ABC Technologies Limited
Annual Financial Report 2023–2024

Company Overview
ABC Technologies Limited is a leading IT services company operating across Asia and Europe.

Financial Highlights
The company reported a strong performance during the financial year 2023–2024.
Total revenue increased to ₹150 Crore compared to ₹120 Crore in the previous year.
Net profit for the year reached ₹35 Crore.

Key Business Events
In Q2 2024, the company merged with XYZ Solutions to expand its cloud services portfolio.
A new AI-powered analytics platform was launched in Q4 2023.

Regional Performance
Asia recorded significant revenue growth due to increased enterprise demand in India.
Europe showed steady expansion with new clients acquired in Germany and France.

Conclusion
Overall performance remains positive with strong financial stability.
